Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q: How do I purchase tickets?

A: You can purchase tickets at the station's ticket counters or through our mobile app.

Q: Are there discounts for seniors and children?

A: Yes, we offer discounts for seniors and children under the age of 12.

Facilities

Our metro stations provide various facilities for your convenience:

  • Restrooms
  • Wi-Fi access
  • Ticket vending machines
  • Information kiosks
